About Me

My name is Connie Jacques and I'm a web developer in training. I'm based in regional NSW and utilise the magic of the internet to work ensure my availability for projects based anywhere in the world. After the better part of a decade working in a busy mixed-practice veterinary clinic in a tiny rural town, it is time for change. Although the work is extremely rewarding with all the feel-good moments one would expect, it is also exhausting and statistically one of the hardest rolls to maintain long-term. The industry on the whole suffers from burnout, chronic staff-shortages and statically has the highest suicide rate by profession nationally. It's time to do something different. So, I'm currently undertaking a Coder Academy bootcamp to earn a Diploma of Information Technology (Web Development) and expect to be ready to join the workforce as a full-stack developer in August 2023. By the time I graduate I will be confident working with html, css, sass, python and javascript on the MERN stack. MY enthusiasm for learning new skills means that I look forward to expanding my skill-base further through real-world experience.
